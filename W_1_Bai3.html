Cosequently. he becomes acquainted with the inner city streets, and realizes the harsh existence of the homeless, and vows to give them a chance in the courts.The Street Lawyer is a great book. It is well written and interesting. Other books by John Grisham include The Firm. The Pelican Brief, and The Client
